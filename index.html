Our Purpose

This area just shows an example of the graphs available. This site will allow you to collect data on salary according to:

  • gender
  • location
  • age

As well as being able to judge a company on their culture and managers developers style based on employee reviews.

There are two key purposes to this concept:

  • For prospective applicants:
    • To judge a potential workplace on more than just the interview.
    • To assess if companies hold the same values as they do before applying/accepting an offer.
    • To view salary differences between group, and ergo, try to level the field.
  • For business':
    • To evaluate their managers performance from anyonomous input from emplyees.
    • To see how their companies culture and management styles correlate within department.
    • To view overall ratings withing the company.
    • To compare overall ratings with competitors results.
